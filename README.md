# 📊 Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard

## 🔹 Project Overview and Objective

This project analyzes customer churn behavior using Python and Power BI. The goal is to identify key factors driving customer churn and provide actionable insights to improve customer retention.

* Understand why customers are leaving
* Identify key churn drivers
* Suggest business strategies to reduce churn

---

## 📂 Dataset

Source: [https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/blastchar/telco-customer-churn]
Initial Data: Raw data was stored in Telco_Customer_Churn_data
* Contains customer demographics, services, billing, and churn status
Cleared Data :[churn_cleaned_data]
---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

* Python (Pandas)
* Power BI

---

## 🔍 Data Cleaning & Preparation

* Converted `TotalCharges` to numeric format
* Handled missing values by removing null entries
* Removed duplicate records
* Converted churn column (Yes/No → 1/0)

---

## 📈 Key Insights

### 1. Churn Rate

* Overall churn rate: **26.6%**
* Indicates significant customer loss

---

### 2. Contract Type Impact

* Month-to-month: **42.7% churn**
* One year: **11.3%**
* Two year: **2.8%**

👉 Customers with short-term contracts are more likely to churn

---

### 3. Payment Method Impact

* Electronic check: **45.3% (highest churn)**
* Credit card / Bank transfer: **~15–17% (lowest)**

👉 Manual payment users are more likely to leave

---

### 4. Pricing Impact

* Churned customers pay more (~74)
* Non-churned customers pay less (~61)

👉 Higher pricing contributes to churn risk

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations (Churn Playbook)

* Offer discounts for long-term contracts to increase commitment
* Encourage auto-payment methods (credit card/bank transfer)
* Provide targeted offers for high-paying customers
* Improve onboarding experience for new users

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

* KPI showing overall churn rate
* Churn by contract type
* Churn by payment method
* Monthly charges comparison (Churned vs Stayed)

---

## 📌 Conclusion

Customer churn is primarily driven by:

* Lack of long-term commitment
* Payment behavior
* High pricing

By targeting these areas, companies can significantly reduce churn and improve retention.
